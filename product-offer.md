# Tombola Masterclass

## Offer summary

Tombola Masterclass is a low-ticket digital guide that teaches a buyer how to plan, structure and confidently anchor a tombola event.

## Positioning

- event management skill
- anchoring mastery
- tradition preservation
- monetizable niche skill

## Core promise

Help the buyer stop guessing and learn the sequence, scripts, planning logic and event-day flow needed to run a clean, enjoyable tombola session.

## Product format

- PDF guide
- scripts included
- templates included
- checklists included

## Audience

Primary audience is younger event-facing buyers who want to learn the skill without being spoken to in insider jargon.

## Pricing

- introductory price: ₦4,500

## Fulfilment model

- collect order requests first
- send payment instructions after order
- deliver digital product online after payment confirmation

## Brand voice

- neutral
- Nigerian but non-tribal
- clear and practical
- not presented as military insider marketing

## Key conversion angles

1. Learn a fading but valuable event skill
2. Become the person who can confidently anchor this segment
3. Use the skill for paid event opportunities
4. Preserve a tradition by learning how to execute it properly
