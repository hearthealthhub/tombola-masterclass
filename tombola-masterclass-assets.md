## 3. Short opening script template

Good evening everyone. We are about to begin our tombola session. Please keep your tickets close, listen carefully, and stay ready as numbers are called. We will keep this fair, clear and lively. Let us begin.

## 4. Winner verification script template

Please hold your ticket clearly for confirmation. Thank you. Confirmed. Congratulations. Kindly step forward for your prize.

## 5. Redraw script template

We have repeated the number and there is no valid response. We will redraw now and continue.

## 6. Calm dispute script template

Let us keep it orderly. We will verify the ticket using the rule already announced. Once confirmed, we continue immediately.
